Story

The story of how the W.E.R Renovators family came to be

W.E.R Renovators is a small renovation company started by two friends, Taran Thompson and Cody Tersigni who left their day jobs and used their combined knowledge to start their own renovation company.

Specializing in duplex conversions, which involve fire-proofing separations between two parts of a house. They now have the knowledge to do full renovations including drywall, flooring, painting, trim, and lighting

Using a small team they can give a new pop to an old room in your home, with free quotes and advice from our in house interior decorator, deciding on a new color for your dinning room is now easier than ever
